# Session Abstract

## Roadmapping for Success: Elevate Your stakeholder buy-in

If you've ever worked on a Salesforce transformation, chances are you've seen a roadmap once – at the kick-off – and then watched it quietly vanish like a post-go-live celebration cake. But seasoned architects know better. They know a well-crafted, high-quality roadmap isn't just a pretty slide – it's a power tool for aligning business ambition with actual technical delivery.

In this interactive session, we'll roll up our sleeves and explore the real-world impact of roadmapping done right. You'll learn how to build and use roadmaps that do more than tick a governance box. We're talking about clear, prioritised, flexible artefacts that help keep stakeholders aligned, delivery focused, and scope creep firmly in its place.

Together, we'll unpack different types of roadmaps, work through a real case study, and dive into collaborative exercises that reveal how a strong roadmap can prevent all-too-common programme pitfalls – like clashing priorities, siloed and disconnected solutions, mismanaged dependencies, and the ever-dreaded "why are we building this again?" moment.

Today's world of AI, Agentforce, and constantly shifting business demands means adaptability is key. With that in mind, you'll also learn how to evolve your roadmap when stakeholders move the goalposts (again), and how to keep execs, product owners, and delivery teams all reading from the same page – even when that page keeps changing.

So if you're ready to sharpen your roadmap game, supercharge your planning chops, and maybe even have a bit of fun along the way, this session is for you. Don't let your roadmap gather digital dust. Join us in this rare opportunity to drive the "why" of upcoming work, bring it to life, and make it work for you!
